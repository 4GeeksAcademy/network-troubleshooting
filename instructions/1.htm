Setting up the network of 4GeeksAcademy

You have completed your first functional network in Packet Tracer. If you have done the previous steps correctly, you will see the "Completion" indicator at 100%. You may need to wait a bit for the configurations to take effect and the indicator to finish.

You can also restart all the devices in a single action to reload the configurations using the "Power Cycle" button. Just remember that in Switch0 and Router0 the configurations must be saved manually as indicated in the instructions. Additionally, with the "Fast forward" button you can advance the simulation time and not have to wait while the devices start.
